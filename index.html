The Food Ingredient Checker allows users to input an ingredient name or upload a picture of an ingredient list to determine its health impact. The application uses API calls to fetch data from a health database, analyzing the ingredients for their healthfulness. Results are displayed in a user-friendly format, highlighting which ingredients are healthy or harmful. This process leverages modern web technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and GSAP for animations.

To find the best ingredients, type the ingredient name into the search bar or upload a picture of the ingredient list. The application fetches and analyzes the data, displaying the health impacts of each ingredient. Look for ingredients marked as healthy in the results and avoid those flagged as harmful.

To input your ingredient list in the Food Ingredient Checker: Search Bar: Type the name of the ingredient directly into the search bar on the main page. Image Upload: Click on the image upload button to select and upload a picture of the ingredient list from packaged food. Both options allow the application to analyze the ingredients and display their health impacts.
